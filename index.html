About geoMe

geoMe is a small webApp that allows you to save a geopositionated place. Nothing more.

Instructions:

1) Save a place: Tap on Save place. When you are geopositionated, tap on the save button (top and rigth corner)

2) See the saved place: Tap on Load saved place. You can see the place on the map or on the Google Street View

This developed by Simon Martin for the sole purpose of develop a small webApp based HTML5 and PhoneGap. And why not admit it, remember where I left my car (ouch!).

geoMe uses: HTML5 based geolocation, Google Maps, HTML5 local storage, Jquery Mobile and HTML5 based cache. Feel free to use the code.
